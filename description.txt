Reduced Mining is a simple mod that reduces the amount of time spent mining so you can go off and work on more important things!

[c/00FF00: Key Features (All are configurable)]
[c/ff00ff:•Increase/Decrease in pickaxe speed. Default: +20%] 
[c/ff00ff:•Chance for ores to drop twice. Default: 20%] 
[c/ff00ff:•Triple Chance. Default: +10% of drop twice value]
